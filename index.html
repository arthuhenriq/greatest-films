Greatest Films

Nightcrawler

A disturbed loner, living on the fringe of society muscles his way into the lucrative underworld of tabloid crime journalism. Scouring the city for crimes of a shocking nature, he is aided by a veteran news director looking to raise station ratings.

Inception

Leonardo DiCaprio plays Dom Cobb, an elite thief who has mastered the art of stealing valuable secrets from people’s dreams and is given the dangerous task of trying to implant an idea into the mind of a corporate CEO.

Prisoners

Keller Dover faces a parent’s worst nightmare when his daughter Anna and her friend go missing. The only lead is a campervan that had been parked on their street. Detective Loki arrests the driver, but a lack of evidence forces Loki to release him. Dover decides to take matters into his own hands.

Taxi Driver

A mentally unstable veteran works as a nighttime taxi driver in New York City, where the perceived decadence and sleaze fuels his urge for violent action.

Parasite

All unemployed, Ki-taek’s family takes peculiar interest in the wealthy and glamorous Parks for their livelihood until they get entangled in an unexpected incident

Whiplash

Andrew, a promising, young music student who specializes in the drums enters a highly competitive conservatory where he clashes with a verbally abusive instructor. Under his ruthless direction, Andrew begins to pursue perfection at any cost—even his humanity.
